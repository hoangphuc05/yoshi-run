About

Four people, one project, and wishes for more time: this is PJSS Productions. "Yoshi Run", while being simple in mechanics, represents the accumulation and fine tuning of website and game design skills learned in this Jan Term class. "Yoshi Run" is meant to be a fun take on the practice of these skills, because who doesn't love a jumping Yoshi?

 

To play the game, use the left and right arrow keys to go left and right respectively, and the up arrow to jump
